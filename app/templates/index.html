Process

Invest 500K Euros (for five years) as a Co-Owner in luxury Real Estate in Portugal for a Golden Visa Residency Permit. We meticulously vet all Co-Owners. Spend 14 days a year in Portugal to maintain Residency Permit. Five Years of Golden Visa Residency entitles you to Citizenship in your sixth year. We at EU Passport via Portugal manage your property and assist in its sale after five years.

Mission

Our mission is to help our clients achieve sovereign flexibility. By investing 500K Euros in real estate in Portugal, after five years, our clients can obtain Portugese citizenship and therefore, be entitled to live, study and work anywhere in the EU. Sovereign flexibility provides the freedom to experience cultures, languages and education and business opportunities that are otherwise only available to the ultra-rich.

Philosophy

Why our EU Passport via Portugal Proposal makes the Most Sense to Obtain EU Citizenship

There are several EU countries offering investment schemes for obtaining EU citizenship. See: BBC news

In the GFC, Portugal knew it needed foreign investment. To attract investment, it developed several paths to citizenship via a Golden Visa.

See: Portuguese Nationality, Second Passport, and Golden Visa.

We believe that the package we promote for Portuguese citizenship is the least time consuming, the least risky, the simplest and in the end, the least costly. All other paths to Portuguese citizenship require substantial effort by the Golden Visa applicant, and a substantial risk of capital loss. While there is an upfront investment expenditure in Portuguese real estate with our package, after five-six years, a Co-Owner can divest. The lux properties purchased will have been improved, and therefore, most likely their value will have increased. At divestiture, the Co-Owners will most likely realise their original property investment and more.

The type of properties purchased is relevant to the plan. It is true that an individual investor with 500K Euros can buy an individual property and go it alone. The investor can obtain the services of lawyers just as we do, and then property managers while they hope for the best that their property will be maintained well enough to receive back their 500K Euros investment in five-six years. But there are few properties available for this price near metropolitan areas, and their condition is questionable as will be their costs for maintenance and improvements.

In our business model, we purchase only lux properties. Since we purchase lux properties with multiple living quarters for 1M-3M Euros with 2-6 Co-Owners, the properties are in excellent condition, and can be made holiday rentals readily. The profits from the rentals will be put back into the property as improvements (avoiding Portuguese income tax for the Co-Owners) so that when the property is sold, most likely the funds put into the property will be realised. The Co-Owners can consider the money spent on the property as temporary for which they receive an EU passport, which will be returned when they receive their citizenship and as they can divest.

Since the Golden Visa requires 14 days spent in Portugal per year, those days can be spent at the Co-Owners' own lux property. With no muss, and no fuss, their investment in the lux property is well looked after. In five-six years, a Co-Owner and their family, including dependent adults are eligible for a Portuguese passport.
